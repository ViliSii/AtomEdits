About me

My name is Atom. I am a 23 year old video editor from Finland. I have been interested in creating videos and editing them myself for a long time. I started video editing as a hobby in 2010 when I was ten years old. It all started from looking at music videos from the MTV channel on TV and making simple home and gaming videos with friends and then looking at the footage in some cheap editing programs with built-in effects and having fun with it.

When I got older I started to get interested in gaming. I started to watch a lot of gaming related videos and montages that had music and visual effects in them which made me view them from an artistic point of view. That made me start to try creating these type of edits myself, and as some time went past I became better and better at it. As I got a little better at editing it slowly became a hobby of mine because I loved to see myself improving and what I could create with my visual thinking skills.
